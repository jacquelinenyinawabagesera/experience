    • Product Management

    Product Management is responsible for driving the development of her
    company’s products and delivering them successfully to the market. A
    product manager’s role typically involves managing the day-to-day
    responsibilities required to execute the strategy and vision set for her
    products and communicating this strategy to all relevant participants
    and stakeholders.

    Ux Research

    IN UX Research I have learnt that User experience (UX) research is the
    process of studying users to understand how they interact with a product
    or service. The goal is to create better designs that meet users' need

    Ux Design

    User experience (UX) design is the process of creating products that are
    easy to use and provide a good experience for users. UX designers work
    on the look and feel of a product, including websites, apps, and other
    services

    Front-End

    Front-end development is the process of creating the user interface for
    a website or web application. It involves using markup languages and
    other tools to design how a page looks and function

    Backend

    Back-end development is the work of building the server-side of a
    website or app, which is everything a user can't see. It involves
    working with databases, APIs, architecture, and server

    Mobile

    Mobile development, also known as app development, is the process of
    creating software applications for mobile devices like smartphones and
    tablets

    Quality assurance

    the maintenance of a desired level of quality in a service or product,
    especially by means of attention to every stage of the process of
    delivery or production.

    Data Science

    Data science is a field that uses data to extract insights and solve
    problems. It combines statistics, computer science, and other
    disciplines to analyze large amounts of data.
